1890

Established as a foundry. Started production of castings for weighting equipment and daily commodities.

1893

Began production of cast iron pipes for water supply. Opened the Erkin-Sai Plant in 1917 and relocated manufacturing.

1897

Changed the corporate name from Zhumatai to Keremet Temirleri (Great Iron Works). Initiated production of waterwell equipment.

1922

Started production of press machines for agro-industrial purposes, and heat-resistant cast iron.

1942

Developed the machine gun TS-KG1 and initiated production and supply to war zone during WWII. At the same time continued devoplement and production of cultivating machines as well as kettle feeders.

1946

Changed corporate name from Keremet Temirleri to Keremet Corporation and entered the construction equipment industry. Began production of brick machines and other construction equipment.
